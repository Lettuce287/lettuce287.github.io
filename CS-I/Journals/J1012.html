Section 1

1.1 Describe the general process for executing addition, regardless of base.

Align all the digits vertically, then add the rightmost column. If the sum is greater than the quantity that can be represented by a single digit, carry 1 over. Repeat until the last digit is reached.

Section 2

2.2 What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with octal numbers?

The sum cannot be 8 or greater. If it is, it's subtracted by 8 and 1 is carried over to the next digit.

Section 3

What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with hexadecimal numbers?

Just like octal, the sum can't be 16 or greater, or else it's subtracted by 16 with 1 carrying over. Numbers past 9 also have to be represented with letters ranging from A-F.

Section 3

What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with binary numbers?

When adding, if the sum is 2 or greater, the number is replaced with a 0 and 1 is carried over to the next place.
